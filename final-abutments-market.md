<p><p>The Final Abutments market is segmented into titanium, steel, ceramic, and zirconia types. Titanium abutments are favored for their strength, biocompatibility, and corrosion resistance. Steel abutments offer durability and cost-effectiveness but may lack aesthetic appeal. Ceramic abutments provide excellent aesthetics and are ideal for anterior applications, while zirconia abutments combine high strength and a tooth-like appearance, making them suitable for both anterior and posterior restorations. Each material caters to different clinical needs and patient preferences in dental implantology.</p></p>
