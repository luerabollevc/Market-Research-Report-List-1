<p><ul><li>Character LCD</li><li>Graphic LCD</li><li>Segmented Glass LCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mono LCD displays are categorized into three main types: Character LCDs, Graphic LCDs, and Segmented Glass LCDs. Character LCDs are used for displaying predefined characters and simple information, ideal for compact applications. Graphic LCDs offer the ability to display images and complex graphics, making them suitable for advanced visual interfaces. Segmented Glass LCDs consist of fixed segments that represent numbers or letters, commonly used in digital clocks and meters due to their simple design and low power consumption.</p></p>
