<p><p>The Protective & Specialty Coatings market features various types, including solvent-borne coatings, water-borne coatings, and powder coatings. Solvent-borne coatings utilize organic solvents for application, offering strong adhesion and durability but may release volatile organic compounds. Water-borne coatings use water as a primary solvent, leading to reduced emissions and easier cleanup while maintaining good performance. Powder coatings, applied as a dry powder and cured under heat, provide a robust finish and are highly eco-friendly with minimal waste.</p></p>
