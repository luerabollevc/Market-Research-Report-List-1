<p><p>The dental gypsum material market comprises three main types: Dental Plaster, Model Dental Stone, and Die Dental Stone. Dental Plaster is primarily used for making preliminary models due to its affordability and ease of use. Model Dental Stone is a stronger, more durable material ideal for producing detailed and accurate dental models used in prosthodontics. Die Dental Stone is the highest quality, used for creating precise replicas of teeth and restorations, ensuring exact fit and function in dental applications.</p></p>
