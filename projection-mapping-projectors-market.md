<p><ul><li>DLP</li><li>LCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Projection mapping projectors use DLP (Digital Light Processing) and LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) technologies to create immersive visual experiences. DLP projectors utilize micro-mirrors for precise light modulation, offering high contrast and vibrant colors, making them ideal for dynamic displays. In contrast, LCD projectors operate using liquid crystals and often provide better color accuracy and brightness uniformity. Both types cater to different applications, influencing the choice based on performance, installation environment, and desired image quality for projection mapping.</p></p>
