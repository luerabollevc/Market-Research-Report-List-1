<p><strong>The Dry Sewage Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage</li><li>Multi-stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry sewage pumps market is categorized into single-stage and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps have one impeller, making them suitable for handling low head applications with high flow rates, ideal for residential or light commercial sewage systems. In contrast, multi-stage pumps consist of multiple impellers, enabling them to achieve higher pressures and are effective for applications requiring elevated discharge heights, such as wastewater treatment plants. Each type serves distinct operational needs based on flow and pressure requirements.</p></p>
