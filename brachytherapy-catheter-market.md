<p><p>The brachytherapy catheter market is primarily segmented into single lumen balloon catheters (SLB) and multi lumen balloon catheters (MLB). SLB catheters feature a single channel for isotopes, making them simpler and more cost-effective for certain treatments. In contrast, MLB catheters incorporate multiple channels, allowing for the simultaneous delivery of different isotopes or treatment modalities, enhancing flexibility and treatment effectiveness. Both types are essential for targeted cancer therapies, improving patient outcomes through localized radiation delivery.</p></p>
