<p><p>Automatic Fare Collection Systems can be categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Magnetic Strip**: These cards use magnetic stripes to store data and are swiped at entry points, offering a simple and cost-effective solution.</p><p>2. **Smart Cards**: Equipped with microprocessors, smart cards allow for secure storage of data and facilitate contactless payments, enhancing passenger convenience.</p><p>3. **Optical Character Recognition (OCR)**: This technology scans printed tickets or passes, automating fare collection without the need for physical cards, enabling rapid processing and improved efficiency.</p></p>
