<p><p>The Life Science Microscopy Devices market comprises three main types: Optical Microscopes, which use visible light and lenses to magnify samples for detailed imaging; Electron Microscopes, which utilize electron beams for higher resolution images at the nanoscale; and Scanning Probe Microscopes, which manipulate a probe over sample surfaces to create topographical maps at atomic resolution. Each type plays a critical role in biological research, diagnostics, and material science, offering unique capabilities for analyzing cellular structures and molecular interactions.</p></p>
