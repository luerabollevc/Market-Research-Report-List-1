<p><p>The knee ligament bracing market is categorized into internal support and external support types. Internal support braces are designed to stabilize the knee by providing support from within, often utilizing adjustable components to customize fit and comfort. These braces typically aid in rehabilitation and offer protection during activities. External support braces, on the other hand, wrap around the outside of the knee, providing stabilization through compression and immobilization, ideal for preventing injuries during sports or high-impact activities.</p></p>
