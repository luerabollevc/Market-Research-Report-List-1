<p><p>The switchable film and glass market includes several types, each utilizing different technologies to modulate light and privacy. Electrochromic switchable films change transparency with electrical charge, while thermochromic films respond to temperature variations. SPD (Suspended Particle Device) films adjust opacity based on applied voltage, allowing for dynamic light control. PDLC (Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal) films alter clarity when voltage is applied, creating privacy on demand. Together, these technologies provide innovative solutions for various applications, including architecture and automotive.</p></p>
