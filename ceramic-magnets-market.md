<p><p>The ceramic magnets market is primarily segmented into permanent ceramic magnets and soft ceramic magnets. Permanent ceramic magnets are known for their high magnetic strength and durability, making them ideal for applications in motors, speakers, and various electronic devices. Soft ceramic magnets, on the other hand, have lower magnetic strength and are easily magnetized and demagnetized, making them suitable for applications like transformers and other electromagnetic devices. Each type serves distinct industrial and consumer needs based on magnetic properties.</p></p>
