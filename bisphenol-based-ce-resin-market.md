<p><p>The bisphenol-based CE resin market comprises three primary types: Bisphenol A (BPA), Bisphenol F (BPF), and Bisphenol E (BPE) types. BPA-type resins are notable for their strength and thermal stability, commonly used in coatings and composites. BPF-type resins offer better chemical resistance and lower viscosity, making them ideal for adhesive applications. BPE-type resins are valued for their enhanced flexibility and lower toxicity, often used in safe consumer products and specialty applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
