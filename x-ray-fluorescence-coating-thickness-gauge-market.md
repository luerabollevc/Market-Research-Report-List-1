<p><p>The X-Ray Fluorescence (XRF) Coating Thickness Gauge is a non-destructive testing instrument used to measure the thickness of coatings on various substrates, such as metals and plastics. It operates by emitting X-rays, which interact with the atoms in the coating and the underlying material, producing characteristic fluorescence that is indicative of the coating's thickness. This technology is widely used in industries like automotive, aerospace, electronics, and manufacturing for quality control and compliance purposes.</p><p>The XRF Coating Thickness Gauge Market is poised for significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 8.9% during the forecast period.
