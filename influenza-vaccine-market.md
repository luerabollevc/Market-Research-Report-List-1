<p><strong>The Influenza Vaccine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trivalent Influenza Vaccine</li><li>Quadrivalent Influenza Vaccine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The influenza vaccine market consists of two primary types: trivalent and quadrivalent vaccines. The trivalent influenza vaccine targets three specific influenza virus strains, typically two A strains and one B strain. In contrast, the quadrivalent influenza vaccine offers broader protection by including an additional B strain, thus covering four virus strains in total. This expanded coverage in the quadrivalent vaccine enhances its effectiveness, particularly during seasons when multiple B strains circulate. Both types aim to reduce the incidence and severity of influenza.</p></p>
