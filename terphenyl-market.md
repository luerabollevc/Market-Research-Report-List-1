<p><p>The Terphenyl market is categorized into four main types based on molecular structure: Ortho-Terphenyl, Meta-Terphenyl, Para-Terphenyl, and Mixtures. Ortho-Terphenyl features two phenyl rings adjacent to a central one, while Meta-Terphenyl has them positioned with one carbon atom separating them. Para-Terphenyl presents a symmetrical arrangement with both phenyl rings across from the central one. The Mixture market consists of various combinations of these isomers, often utilized in diverse applications such as electronics, solvents, and high-temperature materials.</p></p>
