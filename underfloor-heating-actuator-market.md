<p><strong>The Underfloor Heating Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Actuators</li><li>Rotary Actuators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underfloor heating actuators are essential for controlling the flow of warm water or heated air in underfloor heating systems. Linear actuators are used for systems requiring precise, straight-line movement to open or close valves, making them ideal for controlling individual heating zones. In contrast, rotary actuators utilize a rotational motion to operate valves and dampers, often employed in larger installations for efficient heat distribution. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing comfort and energy efficiency in heating systems.</p></p>
