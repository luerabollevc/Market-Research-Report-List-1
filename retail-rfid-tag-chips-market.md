<p><strong>The Retail RFID Tag Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LF RFID Chip</li><li>HF RFID Chip</li><li>UHF RFID Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The retail RFID tag chips market comprises three main types: LF (Low Frequency), HF (High Frequency), and UHF (Ultra High Frequency) RFID chips. LF RFID chips operate at 125-134 kHz, offering short-range communication, ideal for applications like livestock tracking. HF RFID chips function at 13.56 MHz, suitable for access control and smart cards. UHF RFID chips, operating between 300 MHz and 960 MHz, enable long-range reading, making them popular for inventory management and supply chain logistics in retail environments.</p></p>
