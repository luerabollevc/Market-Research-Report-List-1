<p><p>The Rotary Airlock Valves market is primarily categorized into Drop-Through Rotary Valves and Blow-Through Rotary Valves. Drop-Through Rotary Valves facilitate the downward flow of bulk materials, making them ideal for applications requiring gravity discharge. In contrast, Blow-Through Rotary Valves allow for pneumatic conveying, enabling the movement of materials through air pressure. Both types are essential for controlling material flow, managing dust, and maintaining system pressure in various industries, including food processing, pharmaceuticals, and bulk material handling.</p></p>
