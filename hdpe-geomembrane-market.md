<p><strong>The HDPE Geomembrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extrusion</li><li>Calendering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HDPE geomembrane market is divided into two primary types: extrusion and calendaring. Extrusion involves melting HDPE resin and forcing it through a die, resulting in continuous sheets suitable for large-scale applications. In contrast, calendaring involves passing HDPE resin through rollers to produce thinner and more uniform sheets, often utilized in specialized applications. Both methods cater to various environmental containment needs, such as landfill liners and pond liners, ensuring durability and impermeability against liquids and contaminants.</p></p>
