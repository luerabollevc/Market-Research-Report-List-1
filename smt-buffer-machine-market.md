<p><strong>The SMT Buffer Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monorail Buffer Machine</li><li>Dual-rail Buffer Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SMT Buffer Machine market is categorized into two main types: Monorail Buffer Machines and Dual-rail Buffer Machines. Monorail systems offer a single track for the transportation and storage of PCBs, maximizing space efficiency and minimizing footprint. In contrast, Dual-rail Buffer Machines provide enhanced flexibility by using two parallel tracks, allowing for increased throughput and better management of PCB flow. Both types are essential for optimizing the manufacturing process in surface mount technology (SMT) assembly lines.</p></p>
