<p><ul><li>Dry Sinking Fish Feed</li><li>Wet Sinking Fish Feed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sinking fish feed market is primarily divided into two types: dry sinking fish feed and wet sinking fish feed. Dry sinking fish feed consists of pellets or granules that sink to the bottom of the aquatic environment, making it suitable for bottom-feeding fish. It is often favored for its longer shelf life and easier handling. In contrast, wet sinking fish feed is typically moist and is used in specific aquaculture practices, providing a more palatable option but generally has a shorter storage life. Both types serve distinct dietary needs based on fish species and farming strategies.</p></p>
