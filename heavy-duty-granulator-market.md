<p><p>The Heavy Duty Granulator Market is primarily segmented into dry and wet types. Dry granulators process materials without adding moisture, making them suitable for tasks where a dry end product is required, like producing powders for pharmaceuticals or plastics. Wet granulators, on the other hand, incorporate liquids to form granules, which helps in achieving desired particle size and density for applications such as food processing or chemicals. Each type caters to specific industry needs based on product requirements and processing methods.</p></p>
