<p><ul><li>Manual Lubrication Systems</li><li>Automatic Lubrication Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lubrication system market is divided into two main types: manual and automatic lubrication systems. Manual lubrication systems require operators to apply lubricant directly to machinery at scheduled intervals, suitable for smaller or less critical operations. In contrast, automatic lubrication systems deliver consistent lubrication at predetermined rates without manual intervention, enhancing efficiency and minimizing downtime. These systems are ideal for larger or more complex machinery, ensuring optimal performance and reducing wear and tear on equipment, ultimately improving reliability.</p></p>
