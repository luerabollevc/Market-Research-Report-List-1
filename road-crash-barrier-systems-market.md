<p><ul><li>Semi-rigid Barriers</li><li>Rigid Barriers</li><li>Flexible Barriers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The road crash barrier systems market comprises three main types: semi-rigid, rigid, and flexible barriers. Semi-rigid barriers offer moderate stiffness and energy absorption, making them ideal for reducing vehicle impacts without severe damage. Rigid barriers, such as concrete walls, provide maximum protection but can be harsh on vehicles and occupants during a collision. Flexible barriers, often made of materials like metal cables, absorb energy by deforming, minimizing injury and damage. Each type serves distinct safety needs in road design.</p></p>
