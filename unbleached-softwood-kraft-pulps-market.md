<p><p>The Unbleached Softwood Kraft Pulp market comprises three main types: Mechanical Pulp, Semi-Chemical Pulp, and Non-Wood Pulp. Mechanical Pulp is produced through mechanical processes, resulting in high fiber yield but lower strength. Semi-Chemical Pulp combines chemical and mechanical methods, offering better quality and improved properties. Non-Wood Pulp utilizes alternative raw materials like agricultural residues, promoting sustainability. Each type serves specific applications in packaging, tissue, and paper products, catering to diverse industry needs while prioritizing eco-friendly practices.</p></p>
