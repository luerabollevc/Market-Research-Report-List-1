<p><p>The Near Infrared Band Fiber Optical Spectrometer market is categorized into two main types: single mode fiber spectrometers and multimode fiber spectrometers. Single mode fiber spectrometers utilize thin cores to transmit a single light mode, offering high resolution and sensitivity, ideal for long-distance and precise measurements. In contrast, multimode fiber spectrometers allow multiple light modes to propagate, providing higher light throughput but at lower resolution, making them suitable for short-distance applications and cost-effective solutions in various industries.</p></p>
