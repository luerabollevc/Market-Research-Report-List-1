<p><p>Intraoral scanners for digital impressions are categorized into three main types based on their integration and usability: cart-based systems, pod systems, and chair-integrated systems. Cart-based systems offer mobility and flexibility, allowing dental professionals to use them in various settings. Pod systems are compact, portable units that provide quick scanning capabilities without the need for extensive infrastructure. Chair-integrated systems are seamlessly built into dental chairs, enhancing workflow efficiency and patient comfort while minimizing space requirements in the clinic.</p></p>
