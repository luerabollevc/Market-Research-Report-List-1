<p><p>The disinfection services market is categorized into physical and chemical methods. Physical methods include techniques such as heat, ultraviolet (UV) light, and filtration, which effectively eliminate pathogens without chemical agents. Chemical methods involve the use of disinfectants like chlorine, bleach, and alcohol, which target and destroy bacteria and viruses. Each method serves distinct applications, with physical methods often suitable for sensitive environments like laboratories, while chemical methods are commonly used in healthcare facilities and industrial settings for broader pathogen control.</p></p>
