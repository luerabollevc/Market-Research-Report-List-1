<p><p>The Medical Warming Device Market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Electromagnetic Heating** uses electromagnetic waves to generate heat, ensuring uniform temperature distribution for patients requiring warmth. </p><p>2. **Infrared Heating** employs infrared radiation to heat specific areas of the body quickly, enhancing patient comfort during procedures. </p><p>3. **Resistance Heating** utilizes electrical resistance to produce heat, making it effective for maintaining optimal body temperatures in clinical settings. Each method offers unique benefits, catering to different medical needs.</p></p>
