<p><p>The contraceptive drugs and devices market encompasses various methods for preventing pregnancy. Oral contraceptives are hormone-based pills taken daily to inhibit ovulation. Vaginal contraceptives, such as rings or tablets, are inserted into the vagina for localized hormone delivery. Injectable contraceptives provide a longer-lasting hormonal solution, administered periodically through injections. Male and female contraceptive devices include condoms and intrauterine devices (IUDs), respectively, offering barrier and long-term contraceptive options. Each category serves distinct needs and preferences, contributing to a diverse market.</p></p>
