<p><p>The Spinal Cord Stimulator (SCS) device market consists of two main types: implantable and non-implantable devices. Implantable SCS devices are surgically placed within the body, providing long-term relief for chronic pain by delivering electrical pulses to the spinal cord. Non-implantable SCS devices, on the other hand, are external and typically used for temporary pain management or in situations where surgery is not feasible. Both types aim to enhance quality of life for patients suffering from persistent pain conditions.</p></p>
